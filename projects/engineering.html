Project Overview

The Engineering Analysis Toolkit is a Python application developed to demonstrate practical mechanical engineering calculations through interactive software. Rather than performing calculations manually, the toolkit allows users to visualise engineering behaviour while reinforcing key mechanics concepts.

Modules Included

Beam Deflection Shaft Stress Analysis Mohr's Circle Euler Buckling Interactive Visualisation Engineering Calculations

Engineering Concepts

The toolkit applies fundamental principles of mechanics of solids and structural engineering. It includes stress transformation using Mohr's Circle, beam deflection analysis, shaft loading, and Euler's critical buckling theory. These simulations provide intuitive visualisations that support engineering learning and design analysis.
